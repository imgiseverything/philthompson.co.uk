This week's main event
Arsenal v Manchester City, midday, Saturday, BT Sport 1
 
 
Head to head
Danny Welbeck
Fresh from his two-goal salvo for England, new signing Welbeck will add dynamism and pace to the injury-hit Gunners attack.
 
Samir Nasri
Having never scored against his old club since moving to the Etihad in 2011, the marauding Frenchman will be eager to impress.
 
 
  17 – despite scoring fewer than two goals a game on average so far this season, Arsenal have attempted 17 shots a match – more than any other team. Saturday would be the ideal time to sharpen their aim.  
 
    Gunners target title favourites  
    On their march to last season's title, City enjoyed a 6-3 win over Arsenal at the Etihad. Nine months on, the Gunners will be out to prove they've closed the gap on the champions.
